R A WALKER

DEADBEAT

ILLUSTRATION

I set out to create a poster that would visually represent the effects music has on the brain. I used black for the background to suggest an x-ray machine and vibrant colors for the line work to symbolize the whimsical feeling music has on a person. This produced a high contrast that really allowed for the image pop off the page. The work was published in a book Graphic Design Project Selections of American Colleges: Poster Design.
