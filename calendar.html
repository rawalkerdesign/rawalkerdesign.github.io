EVOLUTION CALENDAR

For class, we were assigned to create any kind of calendar we wanted. I made an abstract twelve-month calendar that illustrates the passage of time since the Big Bang. The days of the months are represented through the holes that stitch the sheets together. The entire project was created in Illustrator.
