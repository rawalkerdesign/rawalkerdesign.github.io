TYPOGRAPHIC JUNE

PRINT

This was a self-motivated, self-directed typographic challenge that I undertook during the month of June. With each individual piece I sought to push myself every day over the course of the month to enhance my typographic skills and to experiment with different techniques in order to create unique and visually appealing works of art. I produced both analog and digital pieces that lead to an overall diverse collection once they were all complied and hand stitched into a book.
