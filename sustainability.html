WEBSITE

This is the third semester of an immersive learning class that is creating a variety of promotional material for the Indianapolis Airport Authority. The airport wanted a way to teach the community about their sustainability initiatives, so they sought the help of Ball State students. I was the lead designer on the project, working closely along side a diverse group of majors (Architecture, Computer Science, and Linguistics). We focused on creating a website that informs the user about how the airport uses the rest of their property. That includes a terminal, solar farm, Sodalis Nature Park, a bee apiary, and their recycling program. I designed interactive infographics for the site and crated icons for each of the properties.
